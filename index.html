Market Segments

Waterproofing & Deck Coating Systems

Polycoat Products has the proven coating solutions for contractors, architects and specifiers. We offer a wide range of cost-effective, custom formulated, decorative, single & plural component, aromatic & aliphatic, low VOCs, roller, squeegee, trowel and slurry applied polyurethane and polyurea waterproof coatings for your harsh environmental and service conditions.

Industrial Coatings & Spray Elastomers

Polycoat Products has been a leading manufacturer of customized, high performance Polyurea and Polyurethane coatings for over three decades.

Castable Elastomer Systems

For over three decades, Polycoat Products has been a leading manufacturer of high performance polyurethanes, TDI-Terminated polyester-based prepolymers and more.

Recreation & Decorative Flooring

Polycoat Products has been an industry leader in polyurethane, polyurea, high-performance epoxy, and urethane-based floor surface systems for over 30 years. Technological improvements at Polycoat Products have resulted in recreational binders with high tensile strength, elongation, and Die C tear for multiple environmental situations as well as concrete repair systems for commercial, institutional and industrial floors.

Theme & Prop

Polycoat ThemeCoat® spray applied polyurethane hard coating for polystyrene and low density foams are designed to protect expanded polystyrene, urethane foam, scenery and props from impact. ThemeCoat® provides an impact resistant shell that is sprayed on to add a durable, abrasion resistant finish to most lightweight foams, expanded polystyrenes and most other lightweight substrates.

Raw Materials

We are now manufacturing our own Polyester Polyols used in Rigid Foams. More raw materials will be formulated by our R&D teams.

Poly-Fil™ Flat-Proofing/Tire-Fill

Poly-Fil™ products are two component, simple, reliable and ECONOMICAL liquid Poly-Urethanes. Poly-Fil™ is developed for converting air filled tires into a urethane solid rubber filled tire to maximize vehicle operating efficiency and to minimize downtime associated with flat tires.

Spray Foam Roofing & Interior Insulation

Over the past 25 years General Coatings has developed a reputation as a company that provides exceptional products for industrial, commercial and residential use. Our products are manufactured with an emphasis on quality, ensuring they will perform over the years no matter what the environmental challenges.

Concrete Repair

ChemCo Systems manufactures a diverse selection of polymer systems for use in construction applications. Our products are designed to install, protect or repair architectural and structural concrete, steel, wood as well as both carbon and glass ber composite systems. Over 100 ChemCo products are avail repair, anchor bolts, coatings and sealants, machine bases, old-to-new pours, seismic upgrades, plate bonding reinforcement, spall and void repair, underwater (marine) waterproong with PU chemical grouts and extreme environments.

Construction

The construction of residential, industrial, commercial and other buildings encompasses this market segment. We offer a broad array of products for waterproofing, decorative flooring, and coating products.

Containment & Liners

We manufacture high-strength industrial coatings to provide proven and lasting solutions for containment and lining. Our Polyurea (PUA) polyurea-polyurethane (PUA-PUR) contains superior chemical properties that provide premium physical capabilities.

Defense

Defense sector products are for military weapons and systems designed to operate on land, sea and in the air. Although our product offerings are limited, we continue to invest in the research and development of new products to service this industry.

Industrial & Specialty

For the Industrial sector we offer a multitude of solutions for protection against corrosion, abrasion and explosion. Secondary containment solutions for protection against environmental hazards are available through a broad product range including high-performance polyurea and polyurethane coating systems, anti-static flooring, cast elastomers, epoxy coatings, flooring systems and release agents.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure products are available for transportation systems, communication networks, sewage, water and electric systems. Our comprehensive range of products meet the strict specifications and allow a quick return to service.

Mining & Marine

High-performance and durable products are required for the harsh conditions of mining and marine environments. Our polyurea and hybrid systems offer protection for steel, aluminum, wood, and fiberglass whether submerged, underground or above water.

Oil & Gas

The Oil and Gas sector offers solutions for secondary containment, protective linings and insulation. Our products meet the high standards and stringent environmental regulations that are required for handling, storing and transporting petroleum products.

Transportation

We manufacture a variety of coatings for vehicle protection. This provides a wide range of application solutions including protective linings for truck bed liners, blast mitigation coatings for defense vehicles and abrasion resistance coatings for construction and agricultural vehicles.
